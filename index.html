Hello, I'm Jonathan Berkheim

A passionate and highly motivated problem-solver. I'm currently pursuing a PhD in Chemical Physics and studying to become a certified physics teacher at the Weizmann Institute of Science. I hold an MSc in Physical Chemistry and a BSc in Physics & Chemistry, both from Tel Aviv University. My academic journey bridges scientific research with a deep commitment to education and public engagement. I'm skilled in developing both numerical and analytical models, and equally enthusiastic about translating complex scientific ideas into accessible knowledge. With over a decade of experience in science communication, I've published 200+ articles and conducted 50+ interviews across national media platforms, working at the intersection of science, biographical stories, ethics, politics, and history. As a devoted Wikipedian and unofficial biographer of Israeli academia, I've authored entries on scientific leaders, Israel Prize laureates, and historical figures who shaped Israeli science. Driven by S.C.O.P.E. (Science, Communication, Outreach, Public Knowledge, and Education) I’m committed to expanding access to knowledge, bridging research and society, and inspiring the next generation through teaching, media, and open science.
